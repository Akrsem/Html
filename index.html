    Urban Development in Schwerin

    In Schwerin's Neu Zippendorf district, the property at Magdeburger Strasse 20 spans 19,563 square meters, just 7 kilometers from the city center. Planned for residential and retail use, its orientation towards Berliner Platz adds to its appeal. With 6 buildings, it offers significant development potential, with a maximum building storey of 7 and a height limit of 20 meters. The floor area totals 22,221 square meters, including 6,533 square meters for retail, maximizing land use efficiency. Accessibility is ensured by three access points and approximately 132 parking spaces, making it an attractive prospect for investors and developers.

    Within this project, I played a central role in overseeing various aspects of development. From conducting meticulous due diligence to analyzing location feasibility and market value, my contributions were instrumental in shaping the project's trajectory. Furthermore, I facilitated collaboration with regulatory bodies for B Plan compliance, ensuring adherence to standards and regulations.

    Design and Planning of Residential Developments

    Nestled in Berlin-Köpenick, the Waldstrasse residential project spans 2,042 m² of land, offering around 700 m² of living spaces and a total gross floor area (BGF) of approximately 835 m². Comprising five detached town villas, each blending small-town charm with urban convenience, this development redefines modern urban living.

    Playing a pivotal role in the project, I spearheaded initiatives such as due diligence, location feasibility studies, and market value analysis. Additionally, I ensured seamless coordination with stakeholders and regulatory bodies to maintain compliance with standards and regulations.

    As-Oy-Tietolan-Tähti, Finland

    Tietolan-Tähti was a distinguished precast construction project located in Finland, completed in 2018. This 4-storey building houses 21 luxurious apartments, each featuring a private glazed balcony. Additionally, the building offers 23 parking spaces, including 8 under a roof for added convenience. Our company, ICE, was entrusted with the modeling of reinforcement in precast elements and the preparation of element drawings. I played a key role in the details and management, collaborating closely with seasoned detailers to craft precise precast detail drawings using AutoCAD. Working hand-in-hand with senior structural engineers on the design, we effectively showcased the power of teamwork, successfully bringing this project to life.

    StudentApartments-Kumpula Campus, Finland

    The Student Apartments project at Helsinki University's Kumpula Campus stands as a significant addition to the educational community, providing over 200 student apartments in a generous 12,200 sq.m area. The design thoughtfully incorporates communal spaces. At ICE, we embraced the challenge of precast detailing and preparing precast shop drawings for this extensive project. Using Tekla Structures, we maintained a focus on accuracy and efficiency throughout the design and detailing stages, ensuring that the project met the highest standards of quality and functionality.
