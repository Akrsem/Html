Project Schwerin Details

Schwerin

Situated in close proximity to water bodies and boasting excellent connectivity to public transportation networks, the location holds immense potential for transformation. With meticulous planning and design, the project seeks to leverage these advantages to create an attractive and desirable living environment.

A key aspect of the project is the development of Magdeburger Strasse 20, covering a substantial area of 19,563 square meters. This strategic location, approximately 7 kilometers from Schwerin's city center, holds significance due to its proximity to the inner lake and its accessibility to important landmarks such as the Zippendorferstrand railway station.

The development plan includes the construction of residential buildings and small-scale retail spaces, aimed at enriching the local community and enhancing the neighborhood's appeal. Additionally, provisions for green spaces are integrated into the plan, promoting a harmonious balance between urbanization and environmental sustainability.

With a commitment to completing the project-related development plan by mid-2024, the project team is dedicated to delivering a transformative urban development that not only meets the highest standards but also serves as a landmark project for SEMODU AG. Through meticulous planning, collaboration, and innovation, the project in Schwerin aims to contribute significantly to the city's growth and prosperity while providing an exemplary model for future urban developments.
