Project Köpnick Details

Köpnick

A focal point of the project is the integration of sustainable practices, exemplified by the mandatory KfW 40 nh energy standard. Additionally, the buildings are equipped with state-of-the-art digital amenities, facilitated by our associated company, Wandwall plc. This combination of traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation ensures that our development in Waldstrasse offers residents a comfortable and environmentally conscious living experience.

With its blend of small-town aesthetics and urban convenience, the Köpenick project represents a harmonious synthesis of old-world charm and contemporary living, making it an ideal choice for those seeking a tranquil yet vibrant lifestyle in the heart of Berlin.
